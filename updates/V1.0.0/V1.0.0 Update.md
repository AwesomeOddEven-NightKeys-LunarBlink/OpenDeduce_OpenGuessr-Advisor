# First Release! (V1.0.0)

Welcome to the official launch of **OpenDeduce**, the elite forensic engine designed for professional GeoGuessr and OpenGuessr gameplay. 

This initial release establishes the most robust Bayesian reasoning system available for geolocation deduction, moving the meta from "guessing" to "scientific evidence."

### 🚀 Key Features

*   **Equal-Slice Bayesian Logic**: A probabilistic engine that calculates likelihoods instead of binary filters. It gracefully handles human error and conflicting clues.
*   **The Differentiator (Tie-Breaker)**: An intelligent sub-engine that detects when you are stuck and suggests the exact visual marker (e.g., specific bollard or pole) needed to break the tie.
*   **Fuzzy Search v2**: Advanced character-transposition tolerance (Bigram model) and **Enter-to-Skip** workflow for rapid searching.
*   **Forensic Media Library**: Access to 500+ high-resolution visual archetypes for utility poles, linguistic scripts, and local infrastructure.
*   **Continental Hard-Lock**: Strict filtering that clears out irrelevant continents and causes your suspects to "surge" in probability.
*   **Precision HUD**: Real-time display of probability deltas (+/- %) and a **Confidence Meter** to guide your final guess.

### 🛠️ Technical Specs

*   **Epsilon Shielding**: Prevents `NaN` errors during extreme logic conflicts.
*   **0.1% Safety Floor**: Suspects are never "deleted," allowing you to recover from misidentifying a single clue.
*   **Trace Export**: One-click generation of your full logic chain for verification or sharing.
*   **Ultra-Responsive UI**: Glassmorphic, high-contrast design optimized for mid-game speed.
